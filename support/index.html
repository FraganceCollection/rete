Getting Started
Download Silaro from the App Store or Google Play. You can start browsing and adding fragrances to your collection immediately. No account is required for basic features.
No. The app works fully without an account. Sign in with Google or Apple is optional, and only needed if you want cloud sync or AI-powered features.
Silaro runs on iOS (iPhone and iPad) and Android phones and tablets.
Features
Silaro checks your local weather (temperature, humidity, time of day) and suggests the best fragrances from your collection for different occasions like casual outings, work, dates, or events.
AI analyzes your collection to create a personal fragrance profile. It shows your top fragrance families, seasonal distribution, sophistication metrics, collection health score, and personalized insights about your taste.
Yes. You can share your collection, lists, and analysis as beautiful web pages that anyone can view without the app. Great for sharing gift ideas or comparing collections with friends.
Silaro includes several AI-powered tools: Collection Analysis (fragrance personality profile), Daily Recommendation (weather-based picks), Wearing Advisor (when and how to wear your fragrances), Recommendation Assistant (personalized fragrance suggestions), and Personal Notes (add your own thoughts to any fragrance).
Yes. Your collection, search, and lists work fully offline. Only AI-powered features (recommendations, analysis, wearing advisor) need an internet connection.
Silaro is available in 19 languages including English, Spanish, French, Portuguese, German, Italian, Arabic, Japanese, Korean, and Chinese. AI recommendations respond in your language automatically.
Privacy & Data
Yes. Your collection and personal data are stored locally on your device by default. Cloud sync is optional and uses encrypted connections. We don't track users or sell personal data.
You can delete your account and all cloud data from the app settings, or by visiting our account deletion page. Local data on your device can be removed by uninstalling the app.
