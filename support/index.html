Getting Started

How do I start using Silaro? ‚ñº
Download the app from App Store or Google Play. You can start using it immediately - no account required! The app includes a database of 24,000+ fragrances that works completely offline.
Do I need to create an account? ‚ñº
No! Silaro works perfectly without an account. Sign in with Google or Apple is optional and only needed for cloud sync and AI recommendations.
Is the app free? ‚ñº
Yes! Core features like browsing perfumes, collection management, and search are completely free. Premium features like AI recommendations and weather integration are $4.99/month or $44.99/year.

Features

How do AI recommendations work? ‚ñº
Our AI analyzes your collection, preferences, current weather, and occasion to suggest perfect fragrances. It uses advanced algorithms to understand your taste and recommend new scents you'll love.
Can I use the app offline? ‚ñº
Absolutely! Silaro is designed offline-first. All 24,000+ fragrances, your collection, and search functionality work without internet. Only AI features and weather updates require connection.
How accurate is the perfume database? ‚ñº
Our database includes 24,000+ fragrances with detailed information about notes, accords, ratings, and reviews. Data is sourced from reputable fragrance communities and regularly updated.

Technical

What devices are supported? ‚ñº
iOS 13.0+ (iPhone & iPad) and Android 5.0+ (API Level 21+). The app is optimized for both phones and tablets.
How much storage does the app use? ‚ñº
Approximately 50MB for the app and database. Additional storage is used for cached images, but this is automatically managed and cleaned up.
Is my data safe and private? ‚ñº
Yes! Your data stays on your device by default. Cloud sync is optional and uses encrypted connections. We don't track users or sell personal data.
