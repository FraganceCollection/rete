About Silaro

Silaro is a mobile app for fragrance enthusiasts. It helps you organize your perfume collection, get AI-powered recommendations based on weather and occasion, discover your fragrance personality through collection analysis, and learn about perfumery.

Available in 18 languages, Silaro is designed to work offline-first with your privacy in mind. Your data stays on your device by default.
