Our Story

The Problem We Solved

Fragrance discovery was overwhelming. With thousands of perfumes available, finding your perfect scent felt impossible. Existing tools were either too complex for beginners or too basic for enthusiasts.

We realized that fragrance selection is deeply personal - influenced by weather, mood, occasion, and individual preferences. Traditional recommendations fell short because they ignored this context.

Silaro combines a comprehensive fragrance database with artificial intelligence to provide personalized, weather-aware recommendations. We made fragrance discovery both accessible for beginners and powerful for experts.
