FAQ

Common questions

Silaro is a mobile app for fragrance enthusiasts. It helps you organize your perfume collection, get AI-powered recommendations tailored to the weather and occasion, analyze your fragrance preferences, and learn about perfumery through guided lessons.
Each day, Silaro considers your local weather conditions (temperature, humidity, time of day) and your collection to suggest the best fragrances for different occasions like casual outings, work, dates, or special events.
The analysis gives you a unique fragrance personality profile based on your collection. It shows your top fragrance families, seasonal distribution, sophistication metrics, collection health score, and personalized insights about your taste and style.
