Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Here's how we handle your data.

Last Updated: January 29, 2026

Summary: Silaro is designed with privacy in mind. Your collection data is stored locally on your device by default. Cloud sync and AI features are optional and require your consent. We don't sell your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information (Optional)

When you choose to sign in with Google or Apple, we collect:

  • Name: Your display name from your account
  • Email Address: Used for account identification
  • Profile Picture: Your account avatar (optional)

Sign-in is completely optional. You can use Silaro without creating an account.

1.2 Collection Data

Information about your fragrance preferences:

  • Perfumes in your collection (IDs and metadata)
  • Custom perfumes you create (name, brand, notes)
  • Wishlists and favorites
  • Personal preferences and settings

1.3 Usage Data

Anonymous analytics to improve the app:

  • App usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Search queries (anonymized)
  • Error logs and crash reports
  • Device information (model, OS version)

1.4 Location Data

With your permission, we collect:

  • Approximate location (latitude/longitude) for weather-based recommendations

Location data is only used to fetch weather information and is not stored permanently.

1.5 Advertising Identifiers

For users who consent to personalized advertising:

  • Advertising ID (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android)
  • Ad interaction data

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide Core Features: Manage your collection, search fragrances, create wishlists
  • AI Recommendations: Analyze your preferences to suggest new fragrances (Premium)
  • Weather Integration: Provide weather-appropriate fragrance suggestions
  • Cloud Sync: Backup and sync your data across devices (optional)
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
  • Display Advertising: Show relevant ads to free users
  • Customer Support: Respond to your inquiries and troubleshoot issues

3. Third-Party Services

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

By default, your data is stored locally on your device using secure SQLite databases. This data never leaves your device unless you enable cloud sync.

4.2 Cloud Storage

If you enable cloud sync:

  • Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Stored securely on Firebase (Google Cloud) servers
  • Protected by industry-standard security measures

4.3 Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Analytics Data: Retained for 26 months (Firebase default)
  • Crash Logs: Retained for 90 days
  • Local Data: Retained until you delete the app or clear data

5. Advertising

Free users see advertisements through Google AdMob. You have choices:

  • Personalized Ads: Tailored to your interests (requires consent)
  • Non-Personalized Ads: Generic ads if you decline tracking
  • Ad-Free Experience: Available with Premium subscription

App Tracking Transparency: On iOS, we request your permission before tracking your activity across other apps. You can change this anytime in Settings > Privacy > Tracking.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data
  • Portability: Export your collection data
  • Opt-Out: Decline personalized advertising
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke any permissions you've granted

For California Residents (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what personal information is collected and to request deletion. We do not sell personal information.

For European Residents (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have rights under GDPR including access, rectification, erasure, and data portability. Our legal basis for processing is consent and legitimate interest.

7. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Data may be shared only:

  • With your explicit consent
  • With service providers who assist in app functionality (listed above)
  • To comply with legal obligations or valid legal processes
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property

8. Children's Privacy

Silaro is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own, including the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through:

  • In-app notifications
  • Email (if you're registered)
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date above

11. Delete Your Data

To delete your account and all associated data:

  • Visit our Account Deletion Page
  • Or email us at fragrancecoach.app@gmail.com
