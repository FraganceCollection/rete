Privacy Policy for Silaro

Last updated: August 18, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Silaro collects the following information:

  • Google Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Perfume Collection Data: Information about your perfume collection, wishlist, and preferences stored locally on your device.
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous usage data to improve app performance (via AdMob).
  • Advertising ID: Used to serve personalized advertisements.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the app's core functionality
  • Sync your collection across devices (optional)
  • Show personalized fragrance recommendations
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Improve app performance and user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Your collection data is primarily stored locally on your device using SQLite.
  • Cloud Backup: Optional cloud sync through secure Firebase services.
  • Security: We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Services: For authentication and cloud storage
  • Google AdMob: For advertising services
  • OpenAI: For AI-powered fragrance recommendations
  • WeatherAPI: For weather-based recommendations

5. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use your advertising ID to show personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Data may be shared only:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • With service providers who assist in app functionality

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt out of data collection
  • Export your data

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
