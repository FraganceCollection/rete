What We Store About You

Silaro stores minimal data. Here's exactly what is saved on our servers when you sign in:

Firebase (Cloud Database):

  • User ID — A unique identifier from Google/Apple sign-in
  • Collection — The IDs of perfumes in your collection (not the full perfume data)
  • Custom perfumes — Any fragrances you created manually
  • Lists — Your wishlists and custom lists (perfume IDs only)
  • Settings — Your preferences (language, units, default occasion)
  • Premium status — Whether you have an active subscription
  • Device info — Platform (iOS/Android), device model, and app version
  • Timestamps — Last sync date and last login date

We do NOT store your name, email address, or profile photo on our servers. This information is only kept locally on your device for display purposes.

RevenueCat (Subscription Management):

  • Subscription status (active/expired), plan type, and expiration date
  • Purchase history through Apple/Google (no payment details)

Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics:

  • Anonymous usage events (no personal identifiers)
  • Crash reports (device model, OS version, stack trace)

What Gets Deleted

Immediately Deleted on Request:

  • Your Firebase user account and authentication record
  • All cloud-synced data (collection, lists, settings, custom perfumes)
  • Device information and sync timestamps

Retained Temporarily (up to 30 days):

  • Subscription records in RevenueCat (managed by Apple/Google)
  • Anonymous crash reports (retained for 90 days by Firebase Crashlytics)

Local Data (Deleted by Uninstalling App):

  • Full perfume collection data stored on your device
  • Your display name, email, and profile photo (stored locally only)
  • App settings, cache, and preferences

Alternative: Local Data Only

If you only used Silaro locally (without signing in with Google or Apple), simply uninstalling the app will remove all your data. No additional steps needed.
