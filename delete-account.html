  1. Send an email to our support team using the button below

What We Store

Silaro stores very little about you. If you signed in, we keep:

  • Your perfume collection and wishlists
  • Custom perfumes you created
  • Your app settings and preferences
  • Your subscription status

We do NOT store your name, email, or profile photo on our servers. That information stays only on your device.

What Happens When You Delete

Deleted immediately:

  • Your account and sign-in information
  • All your synced data (collection, lists, settings, custom perfumes)

Deleted within 30 days:

  • Subscription records (managed by Apple/Google)
  • Anonymous error reports

Data on your phone:

  • Uninstalling the app removes everything stored locally on your device

Alternative: Local Data Only

If you only used Silaro locally (without signing in with Google or Apple), simply uninstalling the app will remove all your data. No additional steps needed.
