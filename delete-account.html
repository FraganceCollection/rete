How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to our support team using the button below
  2. Include the email address you used to sign in (Google or Apple)
  3. We'll confirm the deletion within 48 hours
Request Account Deletion

What Data Gets Deleted

Immediately Deleted:

  • Profile information (name, email, photo)
  • Cloud-synced collection and wishlist data
  • App preferences and settings
  • Account authentication records

Local Data (Deleted by Uninstalling App):

  • Perfume collection and wishlist stored locally
  • Personal preferences and notes
  • Local app settings and cache

Data Retention Policy

Most data is deleted immediately upon request. Some records may be retained for up to 30 days for legal compliance:

  • Purchase transaction records (handled by Apple/Google/RevenueCat)
  • Anonymous crash reports for app improvement

No personal information is retained beyond the deletion request.

Alternative: Local Data Only

If you only used Perfumo locally (without signing in with Google or Apple), simply uninstalling the app will remove all your data. No additional steps needed.
